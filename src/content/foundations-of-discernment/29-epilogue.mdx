### Closing Reflection

You began this path 28 days ago, gathering reverent awe, disciplined attention, humble repentance, and steadfast hope. Today, you stand at the loom's edge with more than ideas in hand. You have practices that can keep returning your heart, mind, and habits to Christ.

As you step away from these pages:

- **Continue the Rhythm:** Keep pausing in worship, probing with Scripture, and resting in God’s grace.
- **Share the Story:** Invite others into this path. Mentor a friend, lead a small group, or tell the truth about what Christ has been reordering in you.
- **Stay Teachable:** New questions will arise; treat them as fresh threads to weave into your faithfulness.

This devotional is both a foundation and a launchpad. May each practice you have learned become second nature, not as a technique for self-mastery, but as a steady return to truthful witness in every conversation, decision, and ordinary moment.

May I walk in discernment, speak in love, and bear witness to Christ in the ordinary texture of life. Bless this tapestry of my life, that it might display Your glory to all I meet. In Jesus’ name, Amen.”
